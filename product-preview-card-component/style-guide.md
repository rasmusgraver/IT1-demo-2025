# Front-end Style Guide

## Colors

### Primary

- Green 500: hsl(158, 36%, 37%)
- Green 700: hsl(158, 42%, 18%)

### Neutral

- Black: hsl(212, 21%, 14%)
- Grey: hsl(228, 12%, 48%)
