About Ancient Bond, Endless Grace Horses have stood beside humanity for over 5,000 years — carrying warriors into battle, pulling ploughs across fields, and inspiring artists across every civilisation. There are over 350 recognised breeds, each shaped by the land and people they grew up with. From the desert-bred Arabian to the cold-weather Fjord, environment has sculpted every detail of their form. Today they are athletes, therapists, and companions. They read our moods, mirror our energy, and remind us of something unhurried and wild.
